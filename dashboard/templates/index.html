🎥 Informed Consent Required

This app analyzes your facial expressions using the research-grounded Facial Action Coding System (FACS) to estimate likely emotional and cognitive states. Before continuing, please read:

  • Your camera will turn on. Video frames are sent to the server only for analysis.
  • No raw video is stored. Only derived features and timestamps are logged.
  • Facial-expression inference is probabilistic, not truth. It has known bias across skin tones and cultures.
  • Do NOT use outputs for high-stakes decisions (hiring, medical, legal).
  • You can revoke consent at any time by closing this tab.
