# Ethics, Consent & Limitations

## 🛑 Read before deploying

This system analyzes facial expressions to infer **probable** emotional and
cognitive states. It does **not** read minds, detect lies, or measure truth.

### Scientific limitations
- Barrett et al. (2019) showed facial expressions are NOT reliable, universal
  indicators of inner emotion.
- Accuracy varies by skin tone, age, gender, and culture. Known bias exists.
- Individual baselines vary widely. Personalization is required for accuracy.

### Required practices
1. **Explicit informed consent** before any camera activation.
2. **No covert use.** Never run on someone unaware.
3. **Data minimization.** Do not store raw video frames.
4. **Right to delete.** Users must be able to erase their profile/history.
5. **No high-stakes decisions.** Never use for hiring, lending, policing, or
   medical/legal judgment.

### Compliance notes
- GDPR Art. 9: facial biometrics are "special category" data.
- BIPA (Illinois), CCPA (California) have specific requirements.
- Consult a lawyer before any commercial deployment.
